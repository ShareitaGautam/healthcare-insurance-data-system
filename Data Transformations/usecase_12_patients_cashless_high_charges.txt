TITLE:
Patients with Cashless Insurance and High Medical Charges

DESCRIPTION:
Identify patients who have availed cashless insurance and whose total medical charges are greater than or equal to Rs. 50,000.

EXPLANATION:
This requirement could not be implemented because the available datasets do not contain the required fields such as:
- Cashless insurance indicator (e.g., cashless_flag)
- Total hospital charges (e.g., total_charges or bill_amount)

Without these fields, it is not possible to:
1. Determine whether a patient used cashless insurance.
2. Verify if the total medical charges meet the specified threshold.

Therefore, this requirement cannot be fulfilled with the given data.
