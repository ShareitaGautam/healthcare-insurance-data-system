TITLE:
Female Patients Over Age 40 with Knee Surgery in the Past Year

DESCRIPTION:
Identify female patients who are older than 40 years and have undergone knee surgery within the last one year.

EXPLANATION:
This requirement could not be implemented because the available datasets do not contain:
- Procedure or surgery details (e.g., knee surgery)
- Treatment/admission/surgery date

Although patient gender and birth date are available, the absence of:
1. Surgery/procedure information
2. Date fields to determine time (past year)

makes it impossible to accurately identify patients matching the given criteria.

Therefore, this requirement cannot be fulfilled with the given data.
